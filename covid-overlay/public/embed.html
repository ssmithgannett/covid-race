Deadly Discrimination

Racist policies mean Black, Indigenous, Latino and Asian Americans are poorer and sicker than white Americans. COVID-19 made these inequalities a death sentence.

This USA TODAY investigation puts readers in disadvantaged communities and in the homes of those who have lost loved ones to the pandemic, and shows how the policies of the past and present have made these populations prime targets for COVID-19.
